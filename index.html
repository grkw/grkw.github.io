I'm a Robotics Software Engineer at Intrinsic, a software and AI robotics company at Alphabet.

I'm excited about generalizable, data-efficient embodied intelligence. In particular, I'm interested in applying deep learning and generative models to trajectory planning and decision-making.

My mission is to make robots smart enough to handle the dull, dirty, and dangerous labor that's vital to society — so that people can live and work as humans, not as machines.

I graduated from UCLA with a B.S. in Electrical Engineering in June 2024 as a Regents Scholar. I'm also the author of a sci-fi novel called The Glitch and an alum of the Recurse Center.
